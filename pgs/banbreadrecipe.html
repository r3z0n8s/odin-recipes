Banana Bread Recipe

Description

An almost creamy sweet banana bread recipe that actually melts in your mouth and is great for insfusing!!

Ingredients

  • 3 cup flour
  • 1.5 tsp baking soda
  • 0.75 tsp cinnamon
  • 0.50 tsp salt
  • 0.25 tsp garlic powder
  • 0.75 cup butter
  • 0.33 cup sugar
  • 5 tbs honey+
  • 0.75 cup brown sugar
  • 1.5 tsp vanilla extract
  • 3 egg
  • 1.5 cup chocolote morsel
  • 2.5 cup mashed banana

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
  2. Combine flour, baking soda, cinnamon, and salt in a small bowl.
  3. Beat butter, sugar, brown sugar, and vanilla extract in a large bowl until creamy.
  4. Add eggs, one at a time, to the large bowl beating well while adding until smooth.
  5. Add mashed banana slowly until thoroughly mixed.
  6. Slowly beat flour mixture into large bowl.
  7. Add and stir in morsels.
  8. Bake for 25 minutes, remove from oven, and drizzle a layer of honey across the loaf.
  9. Bake for 45 minutes minutes or until desired color and texture.
  10. Allow cooling for 5-8 minutes, and enjoy.
