Homemade Biscuit Recipe

Description

A savory homemade biscuit recipe for melty biscuits. Crispy on the outside and fluffy on the inside.

Ingredients

  • 2 cup flour
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 2 tsp sugar
  • 0.25 tsp ground parsley
  • 0.33 tsp garlic powder
  • 0.75 cup butter
  • 1.5 cup buttermilk

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees F.
  2. Combine all dry ingredients, except sugar, in a small bowl until well mixed.
  3. Beat butter, sugar, and buttermilk in a large bowl until creamy.
  4. Add dry mixture slowly to the wet mixture until it reaches a smooth dough texture.
  5. For Homestyle appearance, just pull about 0.5 cup pieces from the dough and place on an oiled baking sheet.
  6. For a "neat" appearance, roll 0.5 cup pieces into spheres and place on oiled baking sheet.
  7. Bake for 10-12 minutes or until desired color and texture.
  8. Allow cooling for 3-5 minutes, and enjoy.
